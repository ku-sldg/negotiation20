\begin{itemize} 
	\item[] $\preceq_A, \preceq_T = $ negotiation
	\item[] $\preceq_A \cdot  \preceq_T = $ selection policy. This is a lattice operation. 
	\item[] $E_T =\{e : E | \pi_T(e)\} = $ privacy policy
	\item[] $\pi_T $ and $\pi_A $ are privacy policies for the target and appraiser, respectively. This is called the ``local policy".
\end{itemize}
